Movie Seat Booking

Live Demo GitHub

Problem

Some apps just work better when information can be store locally on the users device. Then when the time is right or the connection is good that data is ready to passed back to the database.

Action

To demonstrate the usefulness of local storage I made a booking app for a drive in movie theater. Users can select where they want to park and those spots turn green. Red sots are already taken and yellow spots are available.

Result

When the user refreshes the browser. They won't lose their spots, because you never know when your browser will glitch or your internet will drop. The app also adds up the cost and number of parking spots you have reserved based on the movie selected.
